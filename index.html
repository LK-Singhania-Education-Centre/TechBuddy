LK Singhania Education Centre



L K Singhania Education Centre came into existence in July 1987 . This centre Is a realization of the value and dreams of the great visionary Late Lala Kamlapat Singhania and the centre is named after him . L K Singhania Education Centre is a 10 + 2 co-educational, residential, English medium School affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi Accommodating 1650 students including 950 boarders. This centre is a Multicultural school where traditions, religions and beliefs are respected and Celebrated.
